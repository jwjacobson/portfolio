Who made this?

I’m Jeff Jacobson, a software developer specializing in building robust back-end systems using Python and Django. With a deep background in the arts and humanities, I love creating apps that let us explore different aspects of human culture, whether it's managing a jazz repertoire with Jazztunes or archiving correspondence with Gfetch! A longtime Linux user, I believe in free software and am excited to finally be able to give something back to the FOSS ecosystem.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me playing and listening to music, reading, or spending time with my family and friends. This portfolio was created using Django, htmx, and Tailwind CSS.

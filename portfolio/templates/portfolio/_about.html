Who made this?

I’m Jeff Jacobson, a software developer specializing in building robust back-end systems using Python and Django. With a deep background in the arts and humanities, I love creating apps that let us explore our culture, whether that's managing a jazz repertoire with Jazztunes or archiving communications with Gfetch! A longtime Linux user, I believe in free software and am excited to finally be able to give something back to the FOSS ecosystem.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me playing music, reading, or spending time with my family. This portfolio was created using Django, htmx, and Tailwind CSS.
